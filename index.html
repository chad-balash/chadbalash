About

"I consider most things to be art...You just have to look harder at somethings than others to see it."

Chad Balash is an executive and co-owner of Express Companies, Inc., the largest US retailer of CPR, First Aid, Disaster Survival, and Safety supplies online. Chad has facilitated streamlining most processes of operation and customer service for Express Companies, Inc.. He has also been a key part in designing and implementing many safety related websites and marketing campaigns that have generated well over 20MM in sales. Thanks to Chad's efforts and those of his Team, Express Companies, Inc. is listed by Inc Magazine as the 73rd fastest growing company in San Diego with a 42% growth rate - even after 20 years.

He is currently pursuing further education in web design and programming so that Express Companies, Inc. will be able to bring all web development in house.
